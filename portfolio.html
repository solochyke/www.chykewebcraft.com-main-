The Solution: A full digital revamp.

AirComfort Experts

The Challenge: Lacking online authority.

The client had a basic website but lacked a content strategy to build authority in their market. They were invisible on Google, making it hard to compete with larger companies.

The Solution: Strategic content and on-page SEO.

We developed a custom WordPress site with an integrated blog to publish high-value content. We also performed comprehensive on-page SEO to target key service pages and long-tail keywords.

The Result: Increased local search rankings.

Within six months, AirComfort Experts began ranking on the first page of Google for critical local search terms like "HVAC repair [City Name]," leading to a **250% increase** in organic traffic.

RapidCool HVAC

The Challenge: High bounce rate and low conversions.

The client's site was confusing for visitors, especially on mobile devices. They had traffic but it wasn't leading to phone calls or form submissions, resulting in a high bounce rate.

The Solution: Mobile-first design and lead capture optimization.

We designed a new, mobile-first website with a simplified user journey. We placed clear, prominent lead capture forms and "click-to-call" buttons on every key page to encourage immediate action.

The Result: Improved user flow and more conversions.

The redesigned site significantly improved the user flow, leading to a **45% drop in bounce rate** and a **double-digit increase** in completed form submissions and phone calls.
